

Features
========
1. Wolken: 
    - unterschiedliche Geschwindigkeiten 
    - Respawn auf unterschiedlichen Y Positionen
    - Geschwindikeitsänderungen der Wolken nach einer Random Zeit auf einer Random Geschwindigkeit angepssst auf die derzeitige Geschwindigkeit

2. Chicken
    - unterschiedliche Geschwindigkeiten 
    - Respawn auf unterschiedlichen Y Positionen
    - Fussbewegungen unterschiedlicher Start
    - Geschwindikeitsänderungen der Chicken nach einer Random Zeit auf einer Random Geschwindigkeit 

3. Al Pollo Bewegungen
    - Gehen
    - Rennen 
    - Springen 
    - Ducken
    - Schlafen Kurz
    - Schlafen lang
    
4. Al Pollo Waffen    
    - auf ein Chicken springen
    - Flaschen als Wurfwaffe E
    - Rollangriff (Ansturm mit sich drehenden Körper) Q

5. Schadennehmen
    - Berühren der Chicken
    - gepickt werden
    - Springender Boss
    - Werfen von coins vom Boss
    - Federregen vom Boss

    but only 50 
4. The chicks can drop also items, either to sell or to use

Bottles:
    - You will need around 200 to defeat the Boss
    - Your inventar of amor is 200;

Coins:
    - you can collect the coins to buy Flasks or Items, that you find 
      in the boxes.


Chicks:        
    - avoid touching the Chicks
    - jumping on Chicks will destroy them
    - throwing bottles on Chicks will damage all Chicks touching the flask
    
    small:
        Live: 10
        attack: 5 Points / Second
    big:
        Live: 20
        attack: 15 Points / Second
    
    jumping Chick:
        will make double Damage on you, be careful



Boss Attacks:
    Planned Total Time: 5 minutes
    Enrage: at 6 Minutes, Boss makes Double Damage;

1. Feather Blizzard 
    Cooldown: 1 Minute (50 - 70 Seconds)
    Duration: 5 Seconds

    Throws 100 feathers or more widthinn 5 Seconds
    The Fethers make lots damage while touching the Character
    You will need to use a immutable Item, that has a CD of 1 Minute also
    or run away

2. Earthquake (50 - 70 Seconds)
    Cooldown: 1 Minute
    Duration: 
        jumping: 5 Seconds ?
        Erthquake: 2 Seconds

    The Boss jumps up and Falls down, the Ground will go up and down for 2 Seconds
    you have to Jump to avoid damage.

3. Pick Attack 
    Cooldown: 30 Seconds (20-40 Seconds)
    Duration: 6 Seconds
    Moves to your Position, and makes 3 Picks
    you will need to go away after Boss Moving to you,
    you will be able to move behinde the Boss to make more damage in that time

4. Chick Mania
    Cooldown: 3min (150-210 Seconds)
    Boss moves away, new Small Chicks come and Attack in salves
    3 salves
    10 seconds colldown  between salves
     5 chicks / Jumpchance of 20%;

    You need to jump on the chicks
